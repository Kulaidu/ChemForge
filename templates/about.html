ChemForge

An offline desktop chemical structure generator Application designed for High School Students and Chemistry Teachers, focusing on ionic and covalent bonding. It serves as an interactive learning tool to simplify and enhance their understanding of chemical compounds.

This application helps students visualize chemical structures, emphasizing the differences between Ionic and Covalent Bonding. Additionally, students can take self-tests to assess their learnings. It provides an intuitive interface where students can explore how elements combine to form compounds, guided by concepts like electron sharing, and transfer.
